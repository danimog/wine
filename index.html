Our Staff

Alessandro is from Vernazza and his family has been in Vernazza for over six generations. He is a professional sommelier of A.I.S. (Associazione Italiana Sommelier), lives and works at Vernazza. Thanks to his job, he wants to promote the culture of his country and to contribuite to maintain a unique landscape.
What will you taste
Wines that Alessandro selects with love and passion from Cinque Terre and Italian areas. A selection of appetizers that perfectly matches with the wines served.
Time and Prices
You can come when you like from 5 to 9 p.m. We don’t make reservations. A tasting of 3 different wines, smaller then a full glass, is starting from 15 euro per person. And after 9 p.m.? Wine, cocktails and oil tastings in the new “Vernazza MIX Experience”
What will you learn
You will find out how Cinque Terre wine is made. Our tasting is both informative and fun. You will learn something on local traditions. You’ll leave us with a much better understanding of Cinque Terre wine and its culture. And most likely, you’ll leave with a big smile on your face!
Location
Excavated from the rocks, along the medieval walls, it’s an elegant, open, exciting and unique location. In the heart of Vernazza. In front of you only the sea.
